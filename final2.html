What are some of the Latest Trends in Web Design?

Parallax Scrolling/Animation

Parallax is an illusion that makes the things closer to you look as if they are going faster, than the things that are further from you. Parallax uses 2D and visual effects to create an illusion, and uses many different backgrounds and designs that all move at different speeds. The backgrounds moving at different speeds can make the user feel as if it is a unending story.

Benefits:

  • It encourages to spend more time looking through the webpage because of the user's interest or curiosity.
  • It can highlight the professionalism in the company or webpage.
  • It can also draw more attention to a variety of users from the feeling of animation.

Disadvantages:

  • It can cause difficulties accessing for older browsers that d not rely on libraries
  • It can be uncomfortable for users who have motion sickness
  • It can be confusing and hard to find certain information on the webpage

Collage Art

Collage Art is art made by a variety images put together, and can range from many different styles such as abstract art and illustrations. Collage Art was originated from Picasso and Braque, from the French verb 'coller' meaning to glue. Still collage art is still well-known as contemporary art and is still a suitable and applicable form of art.

Multimedia Experiences

Multimedia is the combination of visuals, text, videos, and audio in one webpage. Visuals such as images or illustrations are very common in multimedia because without visuals, your website is not going to be eye-catching and can help your users be more intrigued and interested in your page. Visuals help users understand what information you are sharing and can help make your page look more welcoming. Videos, text, and audio can help boost the amount of time on your webpage depending on the user's interaction and interest in your topic.

Multimedia Pros/Benefits

  1. It can cause the user to become more engaged into your webpage by allowing users to understand the text at a their own pace.
  2. It also can increase the amount of time the user spent on your page, and decrease the bounce rate.
  3. It can also make a better use experience.
  4. It can attract a variety of people to your webpage depending on the different sources you use, such as images or vidoes.
  5. It can create a good user interaction with your webpage.

Gaussian Blur

Gaussian Blur has been well-known in web design for many years, but many websites still use this design and technique to emphasize their webpage. Gaussian Blur is using different gradients and images, while adding a blurred focus and making the text stand out.

Gaussian Blur Pros/Benefits

  • Reduces noise in an image
  • Useful for automatic censorship
  • Easy to create or implement

Gaussian Blur Cons/Disadvantages

  • Losses the specific details and contrast in color
  • Can not handle the black-white color scheme well

Geometric Grids are very simple and easy to create, and make a sharp and bold look. Geometric Grids can be integrated in a variety of ways into different designs, and have a lot of flexibility in design.
